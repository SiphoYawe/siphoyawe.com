Every room needs a desk, and this is mine. The rest of this site is the polished surface: projects, pins, magnets, a fridge. This corner is where I think out loud.

What lands here: build notes from things like Chariot and Mina, what cross-chain actually feels like from the inside, talks I am preparing, and the occasional slower note on faith and work. Short, honest, written by hand. No content calendar, no growth hacks.

If you want the scripture-flavoured writing specifically, that lives at [Ready Scribe](https://readyscribe.substack.com). This desk is for everything else.
